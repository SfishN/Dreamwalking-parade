Final Project Idea

Dreamwalking Parade will be an interactive generative art piece developed using p5.js, simulating a recursive system of dream expansion and contagion. Inspired by the surreal procession scenes in the animated film Paprika, this project translates dreams into code: vibrant triangular 'dream fragments' continuously replicate and transform surrounding grey 'reality fragments' through a distance-based contagion mechanism. Rather than treating recursion as a mere visual fractal effect, the work shapes it into a systemic force. This force spreads relentlessly, accelerating until it consumes the structure. Viewers may click to remove fragments or inject new ones, yet such interventions provoke more violent growth, revealing the limitations of individual control within an exponential system. As density escalates, the entire visual field destabilises, suggesting collapse is not a system failure but the inevitable destiny of recursion.
